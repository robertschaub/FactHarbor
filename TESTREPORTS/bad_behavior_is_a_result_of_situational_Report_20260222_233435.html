Limitations

Significant evidence gaps exist regarding long-term behavioral change from situational interventions, the interaction effects between strong situations and strong dispositions, and cross-cultural generalizability of findings derived primarily from Western samples. The theoretical boundary (CB_09) contained only 2 evidence items, limiting synthesis of overarching frameworks that might reconcile experimental and correlational findings.
